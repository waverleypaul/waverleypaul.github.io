About

I am an independent economic researcher and consultant specialising in the use of data analytics and economic modelling to generate insights that support informed decision-making. I use R programming to transform datasets into clear, visual narratives; uncovering the underlying stories that make data actionable.

Drawing on an 18-year career at the Eastern Caribbean Central Bank, I bring extensive expertise in developing evidence-based policy recommendations, preparing technical reports, and compiling and analysing monetary and financial statistics to support strategic decision-making.

As an independent researcher, I combine deep institutional experience with modern analytical tools to transform data into actionable insights designed to strengthen financial systems, support sustainable economic development and guide policymakers and organisations.

I am available for remote consulting engagements, freelance research contracts, and collaborative projects in financial sector analysis, macroprudential policy research, and data visualisation.
